## Common questions

### How do I delete my account?

Open Pythia → **Profile** tab → **Account** → **Delete account**. Private accounts are erased immediately. Standard accounts are anonymized after a 30-day window during which you can sign back in to cancel deletion.

### I lost my recovery code for a Private account

Private accounts have no email or phone backup by design — that's the privacy trade-off. Without the recovery code, the account is unrecoverable. If you'd like to keep a recoverable account, upgrade to Standard from **Profile → Add Apple sign-in** (or sign up with email next time).

### A question I paid for hasn't been answered

Each advisor has a Service-Level deadline shown next to the **Awaiting reply** badge on your Sent tab. If your question is past that deadline, it will be automatically refunded through Apple. Refunds appear in your Apple billing within 1–3 business days. If you'd like to speak to a human about a specific question, email us.

### I think my question was flagged as an emergency by mistake

Pythia runs an on-device check for language suggesting acute risk. The check is conservative on purpose. If you'd like your question delivered to the advisor anyway, email us with a description of the question (do not paste your full question text in email) and we'll review.

### I'm an advisor and my invitation code didn't work

Codes expire after 7 days and can only be used once. Email the founder for a fresh one.
